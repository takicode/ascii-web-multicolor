HOW THE APP WORKS

1. Type your text

Enter any word or sentence in the big text box.

Example: hello world

2. Choose words to color (optional)

In the next input, type the parts of your text you want to highlight. Separate them with commas.

Example: he,rld

3. In the color input, type colors that match each word in order.

Example: green,yellow meaning that he → green rld → yellow

4. Pick a style

Select how your ASCII art should look: standard shadow thinkertoy

5. Click “Generate”

The app transforms your text into ASCII art instantly. See the magic ✨
